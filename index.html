Why FB Messenger?

Reach 1 billion people around the world.

Popularity

There are more than a billion people who use FB Messenger, and 54% of people would rather message a business to ask questions or book appointments than make a phone call.

Speed

It's quick to use, and has a clean, simple interface. Potential customers can contact you with a single click and use FB Messenger to ask questions, book appointments, etc.

Easy-to-use

It only takes a few steps to set things up, and then you will have an inline form embedded in your website, allowing any Facebook user to contact you without even leaving your webpage.

HOW TO

Setup FB Messenger in your website in 3 steps.

  • Step I

    Gather the info

    You should have a facebook developers app id for your Messenger widget. You could get one from here if you don't have one yet. You'll also need the FB Messenger ID of the business, and please make sure it's FB page has messages turned on.

  • Step II

    Include the SDK

    Include the JavaScript SDK on your page once, ideally right after the opening <body> tag. Replace the number after "appId=" with your own app id.

  • Step III

    Add the widget

    Place this code wherever you want the plugin to appear on your page, replace all the "getbroadly" parts with the url of the business's FB Messenger ID. Feel free to modify the "data-width" and "data-height" attributes to fit your needs.
